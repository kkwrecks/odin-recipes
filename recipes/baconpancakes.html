Bacon Pancakes Recipe

Created by: Finn the Dog

Ingredients

(Makes enough for Jake after singing)

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Baking powder and soda
  • 3 tablespoons sugar and salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • loads of smoked bacon, cooked crisp and diced
  • plenty of walnut butter

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy, then dice and set aside.
  2. Combine dry pancake ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) in a large mixing bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together wet ingredients (eggs, buttermilk, melted butter), then slowly add the dry ingredients until mixed thoroughly.
  4. Heat nonstick pan over medium heat and coat with nonstick spray. Using a serving spoon, carefully place a large spoonful of batter on pan, allowing the batter to fall straight off the top of the spoon and naturally form an even circle.
  5. Cook until bottom side is nicely browned, then sprinkle the crisped bacon generously onto the pancake and flip immediately.
  6. Repeat till all batter used up.
  7. Dress the pancakes with as much walnut butter and maple jelly as youâ€™d like and serve immediately.
