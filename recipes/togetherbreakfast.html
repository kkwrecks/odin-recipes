Together Breakfast Recipe

Created by: Steven Universe

Steven makes breakfast at home trying to gather all the Gems to join him.
But it ends up a breakfast that he just cannot share with the others.
But never try never know!

Maybe you can get the Gems together!

Ingredients

(Makes enough to feed everyone who sits down)

  • Stack of waffles
  • 1 Bag of Microwave Popcorn
  • Lots of Maple syrup
  • Whipped Cream
  • 1 Strawberry

Instructions

  1. Toast waffles in a toaster and stack on top of each other. Next, place a bag of microwave popcorn into the microwave and set for whatever time wonâ€™t cause it to burn.
  2. Top waffles with a reasonable amount of syrup and surround the waffles with the popcorn.
  3. Top waffles with whipped cream and a single strawberry.
