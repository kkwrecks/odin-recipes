The Powerpuff Girls Recipe

Created by: Professor Utonium

How to create three super-powered little girls. They will constantly save the world (or at least the city of Townsville) from monsters, would-be conquerers and a few other gross things.

Get to work now!

Ingredients

(Makes 3 PPGs)

  • Abundance of Sugar
  • Plenty of Spice
  • Everything nice
  • Accidental amount of Chemical X

Instructions

  1. Add sugar, spice and everything nice in order into big cauldron. Stir with each ingrdient added.
  2. Cook with relish for extra attitude.
  3. Add more than a splash of Chemical X, by accident or not.
  4. Brace yourself for a chaotic time with three adorables under your roof.
